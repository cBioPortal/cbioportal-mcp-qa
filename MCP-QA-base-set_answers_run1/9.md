### Key Findings:

- **Codon 12 mutations** are predominant, accounting for **66.2%** of all KRAS mutations in colorectal cancer
- **Codon 13 mutations** represent **19.0%** of KRAS mutations  
- **Codon 146 mutations** account for **7.8%**

All identified mutations were missense mutations, which is consistent with the known biology of KRAS oncogenic activation requiring specific amino acid substitutions rather than complete loss of function.
